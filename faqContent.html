What is a Dapp?

A dapp is a decentralized application with an internal utility token that incentivizes user particpation. The tokenization also galvanizes network effects for successfull growth of the dapp. The dapp utility tokens should ideally be of no cost to users of the DAPP; users should be able to acquire tokens by using the dapp. These are the kinds Off Dapps SuperDAO will be releasing soon.

Why is a DAPP better than an app?

Because ideally a decentralized app is owned by a decentralized organization which makes it resilient to take downs. Its also cheaper to maintain and economically attractive to users.

Superneum (SUP) Tokens

What is Superneum?

Superneums are the ultimate form of membership, they provide proof of membership to the DAO. Superneum tokens also serve as proof of participation indicators, requiring active participation in the some of the DAO activities to extract any value from the the DAO. Some of the activities requiring active participation include; check and balance processes, dispute resolution within Supercoms and Minicoms, bounties reallocations and general assessment. A minimum criteria for participation is established before hand for the right to be able to claim excess reward value from dapp product and service pools. Learn more here : http://docs.superdao.io/

What are satellite tokens?

satellite tokens are tokens that are attached to SuperDAO via dapp products and provide rewards/profits to Superneum (SUP) token holders. Satelite tokens are utility tokens that are dapp tokens having immense "use" value within consumer facing dapps. Some reward assets are generated as excess tokens from dapp utility tokens to Superneum (SUP) holders.

How are rewards/profits awarded?

Rewards are accessible to Superneum token holders proportional to amount of SUP tokens held and based on activity within SuperDAO.

What are the different types of rewards/profits?

Holding Superneum tokens as an active participator in SuperDAO activities gives beneficial access to multiple rewards levels in the form of various asset classes. Some reward assets are generated as excess tokens from dapp utility tokens to Superneum (SUP) holders. Others rewards like actual ETHER currency are generated from Dapps like Pokereum decentralized poker and accessible Superneum token holders proportional to amount of SUP tokens held.
