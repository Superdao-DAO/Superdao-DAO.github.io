Superdao

SuperDAO is an Efficient, tiered & reputation based, Ethereum decentralizedautonomous organization governance system for incentivized global collaborative management of disruptive, economical viable dapp ventures.
Superdao enables collaboration of innovators and communities into strong thriving forces. Superdao is a collection of various entities,individuals and autonomous agents c0orperating virtually without any geographical limitations to achieve a common goal and enabled by open source, democratic software, built on Ethereum blockchain.

For this specific implementation, the goal is to create decentralized disruptive products and service alternatives, targeted at high value industries, while providing the highest value possible to the end users of such services.


Governance Model : The Onion model

The multi-tiered onion model provides for a yptographic tokens layer and an internal reputation-based layer called "Merits".
The cryptographic tokens sovereignly governs all affairs, while internal Merits provide for efficient management and development. At the center of the onion model is the onsititutionalDNA which carries the mission, vision and contractual values of the Decentralized Autonomous Organization; providing a system of checks and balances wrapped up in a simple modular hub contract called consensusX.


Tiered Participation

Membership is either determined by the "Merits", reputation earned from impactful contributions to SuperDAO or by the possesion of function specific membership tokens.
Tiered consensus participation is not mutually exclusive (i.e Merit earners can own and vote with tokens). Voting outcomes are based on weighted reputation or token number weight.

While, the former plays a role in the efficient management of the products and services of the DAO, effectively merging the benefits of the efficiency of hierarchical legacy organizations, with the innovative and intelligent swarmimg bahavior of the crowd.
Superdao's software is also a factory for other organizations to leverage our system for their own benefit. Superdao is a viable solution for bootstrapping efficient DAO Organizations. It also has state; meaning it can accomodate a single visionary working alone initially, then progressively boostrapping an organization to thousands of members in stages.
The incentives for membership growth are valuable token rewards in exchange for impactful contribution to the organization's components through a claimable token bounty process.


State

Bootstrapping the Superdao is a delicate process essary to gradually bring the DAO from conception to full crowd governance.
SuperDAO utilizes three defined smart contract enforced states to indicate its stage of development, evolution and governance modes


isBootStrapped:

This is the initial stage of development with just the founder or with a number of developers.
Most of the decisions rest with the benevolent founder with support of early backers and or other contributing developers and general members. During this state the founder can award bounties freely with input from cofounders from the development and marketing bounty budgets to value adding contributors


isFunded:

This DAO state indicates that decisions are made in a democratic transparent manner using reputation votes by commitee of developers, founders and all other parties involved in furthering the state of SuperDAO.
At this state a portion tokens meant for public allocation has been distributed to the public and funds are democratically managed with important checks and balances.
Note : At this stage Crowd based token holder control is not yet activated.


isLive:

This DAO state indicates a post crowd enabled distribution event.
The ultimate decision rest in the hands of token holders but day to day decisions are made by the committee of founders, organizers and developers, with the supreme right of token holders to veto decisions by proposals. Full operational DAO is activated during this DAO state. The committee can award bounties freely, but major vote outcomes of holders serve as the supreme authority regarding what can be repealed or awarded
