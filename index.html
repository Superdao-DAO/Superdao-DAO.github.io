Superdao


Superdao is an efficient, tiered & reputation based, Ethereum decentralized autonomous organization governance system for incentivized, collaborative management, multiple dapp ventures.
Superdao enables collaboration of innovators and communities into strong thriving forces. Superdao is a collection of various entities, individuals and autonomous agents c0orperating virtually without any geographical limitations to achieve a common goal and enabled by open source, democratic software, built on Ethereum blockchain.
For this specific implementation, the goal is to create decentralized disruptive products and service alternatives, targeted at high value industries, while providing the highest value possible to the end users of such services.

Governance Model : The Onion model

The multi-tiered onion model provides for a cryptographic tokens layer and an internal reputation-based layer called "Merits".
The cryptographic tokens sovereignly governs all affairs, while internal Merits provide for efficient management and development. At the center of the onion model is the consititutionalDNA which carries the mission, vision and contractual values of the Decentralized Autonomous Organization; providing a system of checks and balances wrapped up in a simple modular hub contract called consensusX.

Tiered Participation

Membership is either determined by the "Merits", reputation earned from impactful contributions to SuperDAO or by the possesion of function specific membership tokens.
Tiered consensus participation is not mutually exclusive (i.e Merit earners can own and vote with tokens). Voting outcomes are based on weighted reputation or token number weight.
The latter reigns soveregin over the general direction of the organization. While, the former plays a role in the efficient management of the products and services of the DAO, effectively merging the benefits of the efficiency of hierarchical legacy organizations, with the innovative and intelligent swarmimg bahavior of the crowd.
Superdao's software is also a factory for other organizations to leverage our system for their own benefit. Superdao is a viable solution for bootstrapping efficient DAO Organizations. It also has state; meaning it can accomodate a single visionary working alone initially, then progressively boostrapping an organization to thousands of members in stages.
The incentives for membership growth are valuable token rewards in exchange for impactful contribution to the organization's components through a claimable token bounty process.

State

Bootstrapping the Superdao is a delicate process necessary to gradually bring the DAO from conception to full crowd governance.
SuperDAO utilizes three defined smart contract enforced states to indicate its stage of development, evolution and governance modes

isBootStrapped:

This is the initial stage of development with just the founder or with a number of developers.
Most of the decisions rest with the benevolent founder with support of early backers and or other contributing developers and general members. During this state the founder can award bounties freely with input from cofounders from the development and marketing bounty budgets to value adding contributors

isFunded:

This DAO state indicates that decisions are made in a democratic transparent manner using reputation votes by commitee of developers, founders and all other parties involved in furthering the state of SuperDAO.
At this state a portion tokens meant for public allocation has been distributed to the public and funds are democratically managed with important checks and balances.
Note : At this stage Crowd based token holder control is not yet activated.

isLive:

This DAO state indicates a post crowd enabled distribution event.
The ultimate decision rest in the hands of token holders but day to day decisions are made by the committee of founders, organizers and developers, with the supreme right of token holders to veto decisions by proposals. Full operational DAO is activated during this DAO state. The committee can award bounties freely, but major vote outcomes of holders serve as the supreme authority regarding what can be repealed or awarded

Governance

Superdao governance embodies an idea called the "onion model"; a multi-tiered/layered abstraction set that enables interaction and a system of checks and balances from one layer to another.
At the core of this model is the constitutionalDNA which allows for a definition of mission, vision and contractual values of SuperDAO.
These definitions can be referenced within the DAO during an organizational proceeding to enforce rules and deter harmful behavior not conducive to the purpose of the DAO. A multi-heuristic voting process usually follows to finalize such proceedings.


Merits

A little higher above that is the internal reputation-based layer called "Merits".
This layer is a committee (supercom) of ALL active working participant earning "Merits" for impact based contributions. This layer allows fast and efficient decision making in the internals of the DAO using Merit based reputational voting.
Subdivided into "minicoms" or sub committees that specialize in domain specific decision making. These could represent Dapp teams, financial analyst, human resources and so forth. Disputes arising from Minicom consensus are usually finalized by the larger Supercom consensus. Minicoms also provide Supercoms or token holders with critical informed voting insights garnered from their collective domain specialization.
Contributions at the "Merit" layer can be "structured contributions" according to the predefined team member limits and timely compensation. Contributions can also be arbitrary (bounty hunters) working on pre-approved tasks of high impact to SuperDAO's short term or long term goals.

Tokens | Superneums (SUP)

The last Layer is the sovereign token Layer.
This is a slow moving consensus layer that has absolute power over any decision as long as its proposals are not anti-missionary to SuperDAO. This layer utilizes a specialized dynamic quorum that is incentivized by value emitted in the DAO.
Primarily Tokens (Superneums) are the ultimate form of membership, they provide proof of membership to the DAO. Superneum tokens also serve as proof of participation indicators, requiring active participation in the some of the DAO activities to extract any value from the the DAO. Some of the activities requiring active participation include; DAO check and balance processes, Dispute resolution within Supercoms and Minicoms, Bounties reallocations. A minimum criteria for participation is established before hand for the right to be able to claim excess reward value from dapp product and service pools. Learn more here : http://docs.superdao.io/
All three layers work together using its core definitions to moderate or check harmful behavior to the overall.
This way SuperDAO'S system for decentralized governance merges the efficiency of traditional startups with the disruptive nature of incentivized crowd collaboration, solving problems such as; tyranny of the majority, low voting quorum or voter apathy issues and DAO product development efficiency risks.
Governance implementation and deployment to the blockchain is based on stages or state of the DAO.

Community

The community represents the backbone of Superdao comprised of entities of various participatorial functions within the DAO.
They comprised token holders, internal contributors and any other participating entities.


Committees

committees are groups within SuperDAO wih similar characteristics

Supercom

This comprises of every member within SuperDAO that have some amount of Merits (reputation ) enough to have a say during a consensus precedings.
Supercoms as a group are responsible for frequent general organizational decisions by proposal voting using Merits.

Minicom

These are loosely categorized references to sub-grouping of similar users with similar domain expertise.
Minicoms are useful for quickly voting on or recommending specific solutions to proposals with domain specific needs. Example of minicoms are: dapp teams, economic advisors, human resources, financial analyst... e.t.c.
Minicoms are useful to weed out the best ideas from the community to ensure the best proposal outcome for the DAO. Minicoms are also Merit based

Core

Represents the inital founders, participators on all or specific projects and are usually limited to a specific number.
The core team has a structured budget and vesting schedule for each eligible member inside the limit. Core team usually work on structured weekly goals and objectives of the DAO organisation.

Bounty Hunters

These represent arbitary contributors outside the core member limit and outside the core budget.
Bounty hunters can earn tokens, Ether and Merits at anytime by making arbitarily impactful, pre-approved tasks and contribution that further the short or long term goals of the DAO organization.
This will enable incentivised contribution wherever gaps are not covered by core team contributors.
Positive impactful contribution is rewarded with SuperDAO tokens through the bounty claim system and paid out with "bounty payout contract" on approval by a transparent committee or by token holder voting.

Bounty Claim System

Superdao maintains a bounty claim system in which tokens are given as a reward for work done on pre-approved task contributing to the goals of the DAO and or its components.
Contributions must be impactful, valuable and acceptable to the overall Superdao framework, dapps or the primary DAPP (Pokereum Poker) it maintains.
The claim system works on a review-approval system basis, in which anyone with a prior approved contribution history in exchange for more tokens, can review new claims for new rewards, before such a claim is finally approved or rejected by a more established members or commitee of the DAO with similar experience in the area (minicom).
Bounty claims for tokens can be in any category including marketing, community contribution, organization and actual code contributions. A percentage of the budget allocation of all tokens are awarded to short term bounty hunters. See more here
The claim system enables self evaluation in the hopes that close to or honest self evaluations gain 20% extra tokens for the claimant, while dishonest claimants are penalized by a 40% deduction or even outright denial of claim along with the reviewer.
The claim system uses multiple categories, sub categories, types and subtypes filtering to award points, see more here.

Token Holders

Token holders are the ultimate consensus on the DAO according the the principles within the ConstitutionalDNA operating within the systems of checks and balances.
This is a slow moving consensus layer that has absolute power over any decision as long as its proposals are not anti-missionary to SuperDAO. see more here
